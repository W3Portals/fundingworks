How it works

a quick walkthrough for non-profits

1) Apply

To get started, we need to collect some basic infomation about your organization, the concept for your fundraising campaign and all the pertaining collateral to insure that your campaign is a success. After this point, we work with every non-profit to make sure we have a campaign that can connect with an audience.

2) Launch

After the campaign content is in order, you simply need to Link Up your WePay account and you are ready to #LAUNCH.

3) Engage

After you've launched you campaign we provide you with a platform to accept donation pledges (payment pre-approvals) which are a great method to gauge interest in a cause without charging donors before the funding goal is met.

We provide a few social sharing tools to help engage new supporters for your cause. We also track all referral donations so that supporters are encouraged to help share the campaign through various social channels.

4) Campaign deadline/goal

If the funding goal for the campaign is reached by the campaigns deadline, then all the pledges are transacted as payments and the non-profit WePay account will immediately receive the funds.
