Send a Girl to TechGirlz Camp
Coordinator: Tracey Welson-Rossman @ Techgirlz.org
A day camp that empowers middle school girls to become future technology leaders.

UPDATE: March 23, 2012: Thank you SO much for helping us reach our goal! For every $150 additional donation, another middle-school girl will get a scholarship to be sent to our Tech Camp. Please continue to contribute and share.
Thanks again!

This project is to raise funding scholarship for the TechGirlz summer program. The Camp puts the tools and technology into the hands of 6th-8th grade girls and lets them take the lead in creating new ideas and building their own businesses in a supportive, professional environment.

During our week-long day camp, our girls will go from idea generation to presentation of their prototype and business plan. Help send a girl to camp and incubate a future!

More Info @ http://TechGirlz.org
